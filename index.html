Hi,


I am a Research Scientist at Google Research in the Google Accelerated Science team. I build machine learning models over (large datasets of) biological sequences.

I have a Ph.D. from MIT CSAIL, where I worked on machine learning (generative models, perturbation models), supervised by Prof. Tommi Jaakkola.
