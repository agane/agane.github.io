Hi,


I am a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind. I use machine learning for translating between protein sequences and natural language (see ProtNLM).

I have a Ph.D. from MIT CSAIL, where I worked on machine learning (generative models, perturbation models), supervised by Prof. Tommi Jaakkola.
